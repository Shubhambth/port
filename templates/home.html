About

Two disciplines,
one developer.

I'm a Computer Science student at Technocrats Institute of Technology, currently in my 6th semester. Somewhere between coursework and side projects, I found that I care about two very different layers of software: the part the player feels, and the part that keeps everything running underneath.

On the game side, I build with Godot and GDScript — currently deep in a Metroidvania platformer, working through movement systems, combat, and exploration design. On the backend side, I build APIs and services with Django and Node.js, and I'm slowly getting more comfortable with the systems programming and DevOps layer underneath it all.

I'm not pretending to be a senior engineer. I'm a student who ships small, breaks things on purpose to understand them, and is currently leveling up in shader programming and Kubernetes alongside the fundamentals — DSA, operating systems, and database design. This site is an honest snapshot of that progress, not a highlight reel.

Movie Booking Backend System

Node.js Express SQL

A backend system for a movie booking platform, currently under development.
